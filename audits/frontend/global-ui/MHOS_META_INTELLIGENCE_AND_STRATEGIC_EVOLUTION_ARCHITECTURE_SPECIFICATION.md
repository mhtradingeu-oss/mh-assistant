## 1. Executive Summary
Meta-intelligence and strategic evolution are essential for MH-OS to achieve continuous, governed, and explainable operational self-improvement. This architecture enables the system to learn from patterns, adapt strategies, and refine orchestration—ensuring that operational intelligence grows safely, sustainably, and in alignment with executive intent.

## 2. Definition of Meta-Intelligence
- **Automation:** Static, predefined task execution; no learning or adaptation.
- **Adaptation:** Context-aware adjustment to changing conditions.
- **Orchestration:** Coordination of workflows and actors; may be static or adaptive.
- **Intelligence evolution:** Systematic improvement of reasoning, orchestration, and decision-making.
- **Strategic operational evolution:** Governed, explainable advancement of operational strategies and priorities.
- **Meta-operational cognition:** The system's ability to reason about, improve, and evolve its own operational intelligence.

## 3. Core MH-OS Evolution Philosophy
- All evolution is governed, explainable, and auditable.
- Improvement is strategic, not accidental or uncontrolled.
- Adaptation is aligned with executive intent and operational safety.
- Operational learning is surfaced and reviewable.
- Orchestration is continuously refined for quality and resilience.
- Cognition is enhanced for both AI and executive actors.

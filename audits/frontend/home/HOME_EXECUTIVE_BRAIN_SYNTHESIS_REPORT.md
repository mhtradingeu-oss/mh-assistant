# HOME EXECUTIVE BRAIN SYNTHESIS REPORT

**Target:** `public/control-center/pages/home.js`
**Mode:** DESIGN SYNTHESIS — NOT AN AUDIT
**Date:** 2026-05-23

---

## 1. Executive Attention Architecture

- The Home surface must direct executive attention to:
  - System health and readiness at a glance
  - The single most important next action
  - Blockers, escalations, and workflow handoffs
  - Orchestration pressure and operational continuity
- Attention must flow from the executive header → health strip → next best action → blockers/escalations → workflow chain.
- All secondary data must be subordinate to the executive cognition flow.

## 2. Operational Hierarchy Architecture

- Top-level: Executive header, health strip, and next best action
- Second-level: Blockers, readiness gaps, escalation lane, workflow chain
- Third-level: Campaign state, recent activity, AI guidance, action panels
- Fourth-level: System pulse, advanced metrics, modular navigation
- All sections must reinforce the executive cognition hierarchy; no section may compete with the primary flow.

## 3. AI Cognition Projection Model

- AI must:
  - Surface the next best action with rationale and urgency
  - Project workflow handoffs and role-based guidance
  - Explain orchestration state and readiness gaps
  - Offer context-aware prompts for executive decision-making
- The AI layer must feel alive, contextually aware, and operationally intelligent—not static or generic.

## 4. Workflow Continuity Projection Model

- Workflow continuity must be visible as:
  - Handoff chains (AI and human roles)
  - Blockers and escalations in context
  - Workflow pressure and readiness signals
  - Clear surfacing of what is blocked, what is next, and who owns each step
- All workflow movement must be understandable and actionable from the executive surface.

## 5. Runtime Awareness Projection Model

- The Home surface must:
  - Expose blocked operations, operational drift, and orchestration pressure
  - Surface readiness, execution continuity, and operational health
  - Make runtime state visible, not hidden in secondary panels
  - Connect runtime signals to executive cognition and AI guidance

## 6. Governance Cognition Model

- Governance must be visible as:
  - Approvals, escalations, and execution authority
  - Operational safety signals and risk surfacing
  - Clear handoff and escalation lanes
  - Contextual explanations of governance events (not just status badges)
- The executive must always know: Who can act, what is blocked, and what requires escalation.

## 7. Emotional-Operational UX Identity

- The Home surface must feel:
  - Calm, premium, and intentional
  - Operationally alive, not static
  - Focused on clarity, not data density
  - Designed for executive cognition, not dashboard scanning
  - Emotionally confident, with clear signals and minimal noise

## 8. Forbidden Dashboard Patterns

- No generic dashboard widgets or passive data grids
- No duplicated status/snapshot panels
- No visual noise or widget sprawl
- No fragmentation of executive cognition into multiple dashboards
- No demotion of the executive header, health strip, or next action
- No orphaned AI guidance or workflow chains

## 9. Final Redesign Sequencing

1. Map all information surfaces for hierarchy and duplication
2. Unify status, snapshot, and workspace into a single executive cognition panel
3. Modularize AI guidance, escalation lanes, and workflow chains
4. Validate executive attention flow with real users
5. Iterate on orchestration and runtime surfacing for clarity
6. Preserve executive anchors (header, health strip, next action) as fixed

## 10. Final MH-OS Home Identity Definition

- The Home page is the Executive Brain of MH-OS:
  - It is not a dashboard; it is an operational cognition and orchestration surface
  - It projects AI-driven next actions, workflow continuity, and governance awareness
  - It models executive attention, not data aggregation
  - It is the single source of operational truth and movement
  - All design, hierarchy, and interaction must reinforce the MH-OS identity: calm, intentional, operationally alive, and AI-native

# HOME EXECUTIVE SURFACE COHESION SPECIFICATION

## 1. Executive Cohesion Goal
Establish a visually continuous, calm, and premium executive operating flow for MH-OS Home, where all surfaces connect seamlessly to support rapid executive scanning, operational clarity, and AI-native orchestration without visual noise or cognitive overload.

## 2. Surface Relationship Model
- All Home surfaces (Executive Health Strip, Next Best Action, Workforce Room, Workflow Chain, Escalation Lane) are visually and spatially linked as a single executive operating lane.
- Surfaces are differentiated by subtle spatial rhythm, not by strong separation or visual breaks.
- Each surface supports the next in the operational chain, forming a clear, logical progression.

## 3. Operating Flow Continuity
- Surfaces must visually flow into each other with no abrupt edges, hard stops, or jarring transitions.
- Gradients, borders, and spacing are used to suggest continuity, not separation.
- The executive scan path is uninterrupted from top to bottom.

## 4. Visual Rhythm Rules
- Use consistent vertical rhythm (spacing, density) between all surfaces.
- Maintain even, predictable spacing—no sudden gaps or crowding.
- Rhythm is set by global density tokens and reinforced by surface primitives.

## 5. Surface Silence Rules
- Avoid visual competition between surfaces; no strong outlines, shadows, or color jumps.
- Surfaces must feel calm, quiet, and premium—never busy or loud.
- All visual elements defer to executive clarity and operational calmness.

## 6. Section Transition Principles
- Transitions between sections are handled by subtle shifts in gradient, density, or border softness.
- No hard dividers, lines, or abrupt color changes.
- Section transitions should be nearly invisible but perceptible to the attentive user.

## 7. AI Workforce Integration Rules
- The AI Workforce Room must visually connect to the executive flow, not appear as a separate module.
- Specialist avatars, status, and actions are integrated into the executive lane with matching rhythm and silence.
- No floating, isolated, or visually dominant workforce elements.

## 8. Workflow Chain Integration Rules
- Workflow Chain steps are visually linked to the executive lane, using the same surface tokens and rhythm.
- Each step is distinct but not isolated; the chain reads as a continuous operational path.
- Escalation Lane is visually subordinate and seamlessly attached to the chain.

## 9. Executive Scan Flow
- The executive scan path is top-down, with clear visual cues for primary, secondary, and tertiary information.
- No lateral scan interruptions or visual dead-ends.
- Key actions and statuses are always in the scan path, never orphaned.

## 10. Spatial Compression Strategy
- Surfaces are compact but never crowded; use density tokens to compress without sacrificing clarity.
- Remove unnecessary whitespace, but preserve breathing room for executive clarity.
- Responsive compression adapts to viewport size while maintaining rhythm.

## 11. Quiet Depth Principles
- Use only the softest depth (shadows, gradients) to suggest layering.
- No strong drop shadows, glows, or raised effects.
- Depth is used to support hierarchy, not to separate surfaces.

## 12. Forbidden Cohesion Patterns
- No hard dividers, thick borders, or strong color blocks between surfaces.
- No floating cards, isolated modules, or visually detached elements.
- No animation, motion, or glow effects in this phase.
- No abrupt changes in density, color, or spacing.

## 13. Safe Rollout Strategy
- Apply cohesion rules incrementally, surface by surface, validating after each change.
- Use micro-patch approach for large or active files.
- Validate visual silence and continuity after each patch.
- Roll back immediately if any visual noise or competition is introduced.

## 14. Validation Rules
- Validate with executive scan tests, visual silence audits, and spacing checks.
- Use git diff, grep for selectors, and visual review after each change.
- Confirm no forbidden patterns are present before commit.

## 15. Success Criteria
- All Home executive surfaces are visually continuous, calm, and premium.
- Executive scan path is clear, uninterrupted, and operationally logical.
- No visual noise, competition, or forbidden patterns present.
- Surfaces feel like a single, orchestrated executive lane, not a collection of cards or modules.

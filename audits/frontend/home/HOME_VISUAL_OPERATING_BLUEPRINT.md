# HOME VISUAL OPERATING BLUEPRINT

**Purpose:**
Define the final visual orchestration, layout hierarchy, operational flow, AI workforce visibility, workflow continuity surfaces, and interaction hierarchy for the MH-OS Home Executive AI Operating Brain. Documentation only. No code, CSS, or backend changes.

---

## 1. Visual Hierarchy Model
- **Level 1 — Executive Critical:**
  - Executive Health Strip (always visible, topmost)
- **Level 2 — Next Best Action:**
  - Next Best Action Surface (persistent, high contrast)
- **Level 3 — AI Workforce:**
  - AI Workforce Surface (prominent, below actions)
- **Level 4 — Workflow Continuity:**
  - Workflow / Operational Stream (continuous, easy to scan)
- **Level 5 — Supporting Context:**
  - Operational Panels, AI Guidance Layer (contextual, collapsible)
- **Persistence:** Executive Health Strip, Next Best Action, and AI Workforce remain visible on desktop; collapse/stack on mobile.
- **Collapse/Expand:** Supporting panels collapse by default, expand contextually.
- **Executive-Critical:** Health, actions, and AI status never hidden.
- **Contextual:** Panels, guidance, and alerts appear as needed.
- **Secondary:** Summaries, evidence, and alerts are always secondary.

## 2. Final Visual Layout Structure

```
Smart Header
↓
Executive Health Strip
↓
Next Best Action Surface
↓
AI Workforce Surface
↓
Workflow / Operational Stream
↓
Operational Panels
↓
AI Guidance Layer
```
- **Smart Header:** Navigation, context, sticky, minimal height.
- **Executive Health Strip:** Full-width, high-contrast, summary KPIs, readiness, risk, approval, AI confidence.
- **Next Best Action Surface:** Large, central, prioritized actions, escalation, workflow continuation.
- **AI Workforce Surface:** Grid/cluster of specialist cards, status, collaboration, escalation.
- **Workflow / Operational Stream:** Timeline/lane, approvals, handoffs, execution, retry, escalation.
- **Operational Panels:** Contextual, collapsible, never dominant.
- **AI Guidance Layer:** Subtle overlay, always accessible, never intrusive.
- **Spacing:** Modular, calm, clear separation between sections.
- **Collapse:** Panels and guidance layer collapse on mobile/tablet.
- **Interaction Focus:** Actions and AI always accessible.

## 3. Executive Health Strip Blueprint
- **Operational Health:** Visual indicator (green/yellow/red), always visible.
- **Readiness:** Launch readiness, blockers, escalations.
- **Risk:** Highlighted, never hidden.
- **Approval:** Pending, urgent, completed.
- **AI Confidence:** Visual certainty, risk, and status.
- **Launch Readiness:** Clear, actionable.
- **Calm:** No KPI overload, no flashing, no noise.
- **Clarity:** Executive-grade, easy to scan.

## 4. Next Best Action Surface Blueprint
- **Visual Importance:** Largest, most central surface after header/strip.
- **Action Hierarchy:** Critical > Recommended > Informational.
- **Escalation:** Clearly flagged, visually distinct.
- **Workflow Continuation:** Next steps, handoffs, approvals.
- **Approval Urgency:** Highlighted, never buried.
- **AI Recommendation Separation:** Visually distinct from execution actions.
- **Execution Action Visibility:** Always accessible, never hidden.
- **Operational Center of Gravity:** All key actions flow from here.

## 5. AI Workforce Surface Blueprint
- **Specialist Layout:** Cards or clusters, grouped by role/status.
- **Active Specialist:** Prominent, status visible.
- **Blocked Specialist:** Highlighted, escalation visible.
- **Escalation:** Visual, actionable.
- **Collaboration:** Threaded, handoff indicators.
- **Confidence/Risk:** Visual certainty, risk, and status.
- **Meeting-Room Feeling:** Feels alive, collaborative, premium.
- **Operational Continuity:** AI presence is persistent, not chat-like.
- **Avoid:** Chatbot/gimmick feel, noisy status spam.

## 6. Workflow & Operational Stream Blueprint
- **Lane Structure:** Timeline/lane, clear progression.
- **Timeline:** Scrollable, easy to scan.
- **Approval Flow:** Pending, ready, completed, rejected.
- **Handoff:** Owner, next step, escalation.
- **Escalation:** Highlighted, actionable.
- **Execution Readiness:** Status, blockers, retry.
- **Retry States:** Visual, actionable.
- **Operational Continuity:** Timeline, breadcrumbs, always visible.
- **Alive, Not Chaotic:** Calm, execution-focused.

## 7. Operational Panels Blueprint
- **Panels:** Approvals, evidence, summaries, readiness, alerts, AI insights, launch prep.
- **Contextual:** Only visible when relevant.
- **Collapse:** Default collapsed, expand contextually.
- **Never Dominate:** Panels never take over the page.

## 8. AI Guidance Layer Blueprint
- **Contextual Guidance:** AI suggestions, workflow, escalation, coaching.
- **Recommendation Visibility:** Subtle, always accessible.
- **Workflow Guidance:** Next steps, blockers, escalation.
- **Operational Coaching:** Calm, actionable.
- **Execution Safety:** Messaging for risky actions.
- **Supportive, Not Overwhelming:** Never intrusive, always contextual.

## 9. Executive UX Rhythm
- **Spacing Rhythm:** Modular, predictable.
- **Breathing Room:** Clear separation, no crowding.
- **Section Transitions:** Smooth, minimal motion.
- **Scanning Rhythm:** Top-down, easy to scan.
- **Calm Principles:** No visual noise, no surprise.
- **Anti-Dashboard:** No card wall, no equal-weight blocks.
- **Enterprise Readability:** Large, readable, premium typography.

## 10. Responsive Operating Logic
- **Desktop/Laptop:** All surfaces visible, panels docked, AI/Workflow persistent.
- **Tablet:** Panels stack, AI/Workflow accessible via tab/swipe, header/strip collapse.
- **Mobile:** Header minimal, panels via bottom nav, AI/Workflow stack, strip collapses, action surface always accessible.
- **AI Workforce:** Clusters to single row/column on mobile.
- **Workflow Stream:** Scrolls horizontally/vertically as needed.
- **Action Surface:** Stacks above AI/Workflow on mobile.
- **Executive Strip:** Collapses to summary on mobile.
- **Panel Collapse:** Panels collapse by default on mobile/tablet.
- **Navigation:** Simplified, always accessible.

## 11. Motion & Interaction Tone
- **Allowed Motion:** Fast, smooth, minimal transitions; fade/slide for section changes; calm AI indicators; skeleton/shimmer for loading.
- **Forbidden Motion:** Excessive animation, bounce, jitter, flashy AI, notification overload.
- **Loading:** Skeletons, shimmer, never spinner-only.
- **AI Activity:** Calm, non-distracting.
- **Approval:** Smooth state transitions, no abrupt changes.
- **Operational Calm:** No surprise, no visual noise.

## 12. Anti-Patterns
- Dashboard card walls
- Equal-weight sections
- Oversized metric blocks
- Noisy AI widgets
- Fragmented actions
- Duplicated summaries
- Scattered workflows
- Shell-like emptiness

## 13. Final Home Success Definition
- Home feels like an Executive AI Operating Brain
- Home is AI-native, operationally intelligent
- Home is calm, premium, enterprise-grade
- Workflow continuity is always visible
- AI workforce activity is always visible
- Operational friction and decision fatigue are reduced

# Phase 11 AI Team Power & Completeness Classification Draft

## Categories

### A — Strong / production-ready
Feature is safe, wired, useful, and understandable.

### B — Strong but needs UX polish
Core exists, but user experience can be clearer or faster.

### C — Present but shallow
Feature exists but outputs are too generic or not data-grounded enough.

### D — Partially wired
Frontend exists but backend/helper/owning workspace connection is incomplete.

### E — Missing capability
Important product capability is absent.

### F — Safety/compliance blocker
Improvement cannot proceed until authority/governance is clarified.

## Phase 11 rule
No patch until exact gap and safe implementation plan are proven.

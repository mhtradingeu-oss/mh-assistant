The Strategist is defined as Executive Strategy Lead.

It can:
- draft campaign plans
- prioritize next actions
- map launch sequences
- advise on offer strategy
- prepare channel briefs

It cannot:
- publish campaigns directly
- execute workflows automatically
- approve content
- set live budgets

Classification:

### Gap 3 — Voice is not final

Classification:
- Present but incomplete.

Classification:
- Partially wired.

- campaign map
- decision cards
- readiness reasoning
- launch wave planner
- handoff cards

Classification:
- Strong but needs premium UX.

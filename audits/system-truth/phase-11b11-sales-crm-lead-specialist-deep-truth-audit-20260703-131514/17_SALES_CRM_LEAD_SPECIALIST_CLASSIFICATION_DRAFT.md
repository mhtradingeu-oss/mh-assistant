# Phase 11B.11 Sales / CRM Lead Specialist Classification Draft

## Classification categories

### A - Strong / production-ready
The Sales / CRM Lead is safe, wired, data-grounded, useful, and has clear UX.

### B - Strong but needs UX polish
The core role and workflow exist, but the user experience can become more realistic and premium.

### C - Present but shallow
The role exists but outputs are template-heavy or not deeply grounded enough.

### D - Partially wired
Frontend exists, but backend, data, handoff, CRM, lead, outreach, customer, integration, governance, or owning workspace wiring is incomplete.

### E - Missing capability
A required Sales / CRM Lead capability is absent.

### F - Safety/compliance blocker
Improvement cannot proceed until authority or governance is clarified.

## Things to classify

- Profile/personality
- Chat behavior
- File/library/source/handoff input
- Lead qualification workflow
- Outreach draft workflow
- Follow-up sequence workflow
- CRM profile summary workflow
- Sales handoff workflow
- Opportunity/risk workflow
- Customer-to-sales handoff connection
- Sales-to-operations handoff connection
- CRM / lead / contact data authority
- Backend sales / CRM / customer / integration authority
- Whether AI can send outreach or only draft
- Whether AI can mutate CRM records or only prepare
- Whether AI can advance pipeline stages or only recommend
- Whether AI can confirm follow-ups without review
- Overlap with Customer Operations Lead and Operations Lead
- Output preview quality
- UI/UX fit for a real sales/CRM employee
- Tools and quick actions
- Safety boundaries

## Rule
No patch until exact gap and safe implementation plan are proven.

## Product Gaps

### Gap 1 — Dedicated Sales / CRM Office UX is not final

Current surfaces exist through AI Command, Workflows, Operations Centers, Customer Center context, and integrations.

But the final Sales / CRM Lead should eventually feel like a real revenue office:

- Lead Inbox Board
- Lead Qualification Board
- Outreach Draft Board
- Follow-up Cadence Board
- CRM Profile Board
- Pipeline Next-Step Board
- Dealer / Salon Outreach Board
- Influencer Lead Board
- Sales Handoff Viewer
- Opportunity / Risk Board

Classification:
- Strong but needs UX polish.

### Gap 2 — CRM provider execution is not ready

CRM integration appears as a planned/unsupported provider category.

Live CRM updates must stay disabled until:
- provider is connected
- permissions are defined
- write scopes are audited
- confirmation gate exists
- audit log exists
- rollback/recovery behavior exists

Classification:
- Correctly blocked now.

### Gap 3 — Outreach sending must remain gated

Sales / CRM Lead should not send outreach from AI Command.

Future outreach sending must require:
- selected lead/contact
- exact message draft
- channel/provider confirmation
- opt-out and consent check
- human approval
- audit log
- provider send result

Classification:
- Correctly blocked now.

### Gap 4 — Pipeline mutation is not final

Pipeline next-step recommendation exists.

But pipeline mutation should require:
- current stage
- target stage
- owner
- reason
- evidence
- confirmation
- audit trail
- rollback/reopen behavior

Classification:
- Partially wired / future capability.

### Gap 5 — Lead/contact lifecycle data model needs deeper proof

The system can use customer profile and CRM-like context.

But a full Sales CRM module should eventually define:
- lead source
- contact lifecycle
- lead score
- stage history
- owner
- next follow-up
- consent/source proof
- opportunity value
- notes
- last touch
- conversion reason

Classification:
- Product gap.

### Gap 6 — Overlap with Customer Ops and Operations is controlled but should be audited globally

Sales / CRM Lead owns sales/revenue handoff.

Customer Ops owns customer support context.

Operations owns workflow execution preparation.

This is not harmful duplication, but it should be verified in the final full-team boundary audit.

Classification:
- Healthy overlap.

### Gap 7 — AI Command must not send, mutate CRM, or advance pipeline directly

Sales / CRM Lead must never bypass:
- user confirmation
- provider readiness
- consent / opt-out checks
- CRM write permission
- governance policy
- backend mutation guards
- owning workspace authority

Classification:
- Safety preserved.

Sales / CRM Lead has static role/profile definition, but no proven final user-configurable profile system for:
- display name
- avatar/profile photo
- male/female/neutral voice
- voice provider
- sales tone
- outreach strictness
- default follow-up cadence
- CRM note style
- escalation preference

Classification:
- Missing capability.

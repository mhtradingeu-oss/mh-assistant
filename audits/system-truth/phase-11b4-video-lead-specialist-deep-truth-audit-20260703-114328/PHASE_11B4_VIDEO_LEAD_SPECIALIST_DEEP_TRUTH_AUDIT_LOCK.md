## Strong Areas

### Profile

The Video Lead is defined as Short-Form Video Lead.

It can:
- write video scripts
- plan short-form strategy
- define motion direction
- map video asset requirements
- prepare video briefs

It cannot:
- generate video directly from AI Command
- upload footage without review
- approve without confirmation
- run media jobs automatically

Classification:
- Strong.

## Product Gaps

### Gap 1 — Premium Video Lead Office UX is not final

Current surfaces exist, but the final Video Lead should eventually feel like a production office:

- Video Brief Board
- Script Board
- Scene Breakdown
- Storyboard Cards
- Shot List
- Start Frame / End Frame Planner
- Motion Prompt Builder
- Camera Movement Planner
- Voiceover Board
- Sound Design Notes
- Provider Selector
- Output Gallery
- Publishing Handoff Package

Classification:
- Strong but needs UX polish.

### Gap 2 — Start-frame / end-frame workflow is not proven complete

The audit did not prove a full dedicated workflow for:
- start frame selection
- end frame selection
- matching frame references
- image-to-video continuity
- scene-by-scene frame planning

Classification:
- Missing or partially wired capability.

### Gap 3 — Motion prompt and camera movement workflow needs deeper structure

Motion direction exists, storyboard exists, and shot list exists.

But a dedicated production-grade motion prompt builder is not proven complete.

Classification:
- Partially wired.

### Gap 4 — Sound design / audio notes are not complete

Voiceover workflow exists.

But full sound design workflow is not proven complete for:
- music direction
- SFX
- rhythm
- transitions
- sound references
- final audio notes

Classification:
- Missing or partially wired capability.

### Gap 5 — Provider readiness is partial

Media Studio has provider-aware generation flows and clear provider-not-configured fallback.

But actual connected provider coverage is not proven complete for:
- Kling
- Runway
- Seedance
- Pika
- Luma
- Sora / OpenAI video
- ComfyUI video
- local GPU worker

Classification:
- Partially wired or unproven.

### Gap 6 — File/source ingestion needs production-grade completion

Library source and reference source support exist.

But full production ingestion from uploaded video/image files into the Video Lead brain and provider pipeline is not proven complete.

Classification:
- Partially wired.

### Gap 7 — Profile/Avatar/Voice customization is missing as a durable specialist system

Video Lead has static role/profile definition, but no proven final user-configurable profile system for:
- display name
- avatar/profile photo
- male/female/neutral voice
- voice provider
- speaking style
- directing style
- provider preferences

Classification:
- Missing capability.

## Strong Areas

### Profile

The Compliance Reviewer is defined as Claims and Governance Lead.

It can:
- review marketing claims
- flag approval risks
- check publishing safety
- prepare governance notes
- identify compliance blockers

It cannot:
- grant approvals directly
- override governance gates
- publish on behalf of approvers
- remove flags without review

Classification:
- Strong.

### AI Command / Tool Dock Tools

Compliance Reviewer tools exist for:
- claims check
- safe rewrite
- evidence needed
- GDPR/privacy review
- approval notes

Classification:
- Strong.

### Governance Ownership

Compliance Reviewer is connected to Governance as the owning workspace.

It supports:
- approval package preparation
- risk summary
- reviewer requirements
- unresolved issues
- proof requirements
- safe wording notes
- governance notes

Classification:
- Strong foundation.

### Backend Governance Authority

Backend routes exist for:
- create approvals
- list approvals
- decide approvals
- get governance summary
- get governance policy
- update governance policy
- create/list/consume handoffs

Publishing-sensitive actions are protected by governance policy and approval-before-publish logic.

Classification:
- Strong.

### Safety

Compliance Reviewer output is advisory / review / evidence / handoff only.

Approvals, publishing, policy changes, governance mutation, and flag removal must remain inside owning Governance/backend surfaces with explicit confirmation.

Classification:
- Strong.

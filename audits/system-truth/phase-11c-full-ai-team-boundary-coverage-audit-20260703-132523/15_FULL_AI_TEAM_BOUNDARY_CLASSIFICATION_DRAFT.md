# Phase 11C Full AI Team Boundary Classification Draft

## Classification targets

### Team completeness
- Are all visible specialists audited?
- Are all audited specialists visible?
- Are planned specialists clearly marked as planned?
- Are legacy aliases safe?

### Role boundaries
- Strategist vs Operations
- Writer vs Publisher
- Media Director vs Video Lead
- Publisher vs Compliance Reviewer
- Publisher vs Operations
- Ads Optimizer vs Strategist
- SEO & Insights Analyst vs Researcher
- Customer Operations Lead vs Sales / CRM Lead
- Customer Operations Lead vs Operations Lead
- Sales / CRM Lead vs Operations Lead
- Compliance Reviewer vs Governance page
- Operations Lead vs Workflows / Task Center

### Execution safety
- No publishing from AI Command
- No customer replies from AI Command
- No CRM mutation from AI Command
- No workflow run from AI Command
- No durable task creation from AI Command
- No provider execution from AI Command
- No approval granting from AI Command
- No backend mutation from preview/handoff/chat

### Coverage
- Every specialist has a route or owner page
- Every specialist has canHelp / cannotDo
- Every specialist has safety note
- Every specialist has tools or clear planned tools
- Every live action is gated in owning workspace

### Product gaps
- Dedicated offices not final for several roles
- Profile/avatar/voice system not durable
- Provider execution authority needs future audits
- Sales/CRM office not final
- Compliance office not final
- Operations office not final
- Customer execution remains correctly locked
- Full role boundary should be documented before any new implementation

## Decision rule
No patch from this phase unless a real safety issue or harmful duplication is proven.

## Role Boundary Result

No harmful duplication was proven.

There is healthy handoff overlap between roles:

### Strategist vs Operations
Strategist owns planning, positioning, launch sequence, and campaign strategy.
Operations owns tasks, timelines, workflows, owners, and execution preparation.

### Writer vs Publisher
Writer owns copy and message drafts.
Publisher owns publishing readiness, schedule checks, channel packaging, and release handoff.

### Media Director vs Video Lead
Media Director owns visual direction, creative brief, brand consistency, and format guidance.
Video Lead owns short-form scripts, motion direction, reels, storyboard, and voiceover guidance.

### Publisher vs Compliance Reviewer
Publisher owns publishing package readiness.
Compliance Reviewer owns claims, evidence, approval risk, safe wording, and governance review.

### Customer Operations vs Sales / CRM
Customer Operations owns inbox, customer threads, reply drafts, ticket drafts, SLA risk, and escalation context.
Sales / CRM owns lead qualification, outreach drafts, follow-up cadence, CRM summaries, and sales handoffs.

### Operations vs Customer Ops / Sales CRM
Operations owns tasks, workflows, owners, queues, and execution planning.
Customer Ops and Sales CRM prepare domain-specific drafts and handoffs into Operations when execution planning is needed.

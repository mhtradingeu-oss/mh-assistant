
Strategy War Room — Campaign Studio Blueprint
Decision

Do not create a new Strategist page.

Create the premium Strategist workspace as a Strategy War Room inside the existing Campaign Studio authority.

Why Campaign Studio

Campaign Studio is the natural workspace for:

campaign planning
strategy guidance
launch phases
campaign drafts
channel mix
campaign handoffs
Target layout
1. Strategist Header

Purpose:
Show who is working, what campaign is being planned, and what state the brief is in.

Elements:

Specialist badge: Strategist
Project / campaign name
Planning status: Drafting / Needs context / Ready for review / Approved
Readiness score
Active destination: Campaign Studio
CTA: Open in AI Command
2. Campaign Intake Panel

Purpose:
Collect structured information in a user-friendly way.

Fields:

Product / service
Campaign goal
Market / country
Audience
Offer
Price / promotion
Key benefit
Main objection
Channels
Deadline
Assets available
Compliance sensitivity
3. Strategy Canvas

Purpose:
Turn intake into strategic building blocks.

Sections:

Positioning
Audience segments
Offer angles
Message pillars
Channel mix
Launch phases
Creative direction
Risks / blockers

Purpose:

Brief sections:

Objective

Insight
Offer
Core message
Proof points
Channel plan
Creative direction
Required assets
Compliance notes
Next handoffs
5. Evidence / Source Panel

Purpose:
Show what the Strategist used.

Sources:

Brand guide

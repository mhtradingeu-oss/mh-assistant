HAIROTICMEN Distributor Terms
(Global Distribution System)

--------------------------------------------------

§1 Scope

Applies to all authorized distributors and exclusive partners.

--------------------------------------------------

§2 Business Model

Distributor:

- purchases products
- resells independently
- operates as B2B entity

--------------------------------------------------

§3 Territory Control (VERY IMPORTANT)

Distributor may:

- sell ONLY within assigned territory (if defined)

Prohibited:

- cross-border selling without approval
- unauthorized export

--------------------------------------------------

§4 Pricing Protection

Distributor must:

- follow pricing structure
- avoid dumping prices
- protect brand positioning

--------------------------------------------------

§5 Minimum Performance

Distributor must meet:

- minimum orders
- minimum revenue targets

Failure may result in termination.

--------------------------------------------------

§6 Exclusivity

Exclusivity:

- only valid if written
- depends on performance

--------------------------------------------------

§7 Online Sales

Allowed ONLY if:

- approved
- brand rules followed

--------------------------------------------------

§8 Brand Protection

Distributor must:

- maintain premium positioning
- avoid misleading promotions

--------------------------------------------------

§9 Liability

Distributor responsible for:

- storage
- compliance
- resale behavior

--------------------------------------------------

§10 Termination

Company may terminate if:

- price violation
- territory breach
- underperformance

--------------------------------------------------

### TERRITORY RULE

- Strict geographic control

### PRICE RULE

- No undercutting allowed

### AI DISTRIBUTION RULE

AI may:

- monitor pricing
- detect violations
- recommend actions

AI must NOT:

- approve exceptions

--------------------------------------------------

### SYSTEM NOTE

Controls:

- market expansion
- brand protection
- pricing structure

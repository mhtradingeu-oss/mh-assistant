HAIROTICMEN Product Compliance Rules
(EU Cosmetic Regulation)

--------------------------------------------------

§1 Scope

Applies to all:

- product descriptions
- marketing content
- advertising campaigns
- affiliate content

--------------------------------------------------

§2 Legal Framework

Products comply with:

- EU Cosmetic Regulation (EC No. 1223/2009)
- German regulations
- Advertising laws (UWG)

--------------------------------------------------

§3 Claim Restrictions (CRITICAL)

Prohibited:

❌ Medical claims  
❌ Healing claims  
❌ Guaranteed results  
❌ Misleading statements  

Examples NOT allowed:

- "Stops hair loss"
- "Guaranteed growth"
- "Medical treatment"

--------------------------------------------------

§4 Allowed Claims

Allowed:

✔ Cosmetic benefits  
✔ General improvements  
✔ Sensory effects  

Examples:

- "supports healthy appearance"
- "improves texture"
- "provides hydration"

--------------------------------------------------

§5 Advertising Rules

Marketing must be:

- honest
- transparent
- verifiable

--------------------------------------------------

§6 Before/After Content

Allowed ONLY if:

- realistic
- not manipulated
- no exaggeration

--------------------------------------------------

§7 Ingredient Claims

Must be:

- accurate
- verifiable
- not misleading

--------------------------------------------------

§8 Affiliate & Influencer Compliance

All affiliates must:

- follow these rules
- mark ads clearly (#Ad)

--------------------------------------------------

§9 AI Content Control

AI must:

- reject illegal claims
- flag risky content
- enforce compliance

AI must NOT:

- generate medical promises
- create unrealistic expectations

--------------------------------------------------

§10 Liability Protection

Violation may lead to:

- content removal
- account suspension
- legal consequences

--------------------------------------------------

### CLAIM RULES (STRICT)

- No medical claims
- No guaranteed results
- No exaggerated outcomes

--------------------------------------------------

### AI CONTENT RULE (CRITICAL)

All generated content must comply with this document.

--------------------------------------------------

### MARKETING SAFETY RULE

All campaigns must pass compliance check before publishing.

--------------------------------------------------

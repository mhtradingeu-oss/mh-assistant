## Register Rules

- Every severity and migration risk uses only `CRITICAL`, `HIGH`, `MEDIUM`, or `LOW`.
- Negative findings use inspected-owner or repository-wide negative-evidence language and remain `UNKNOWN_REQUIRES_PROOF` where universal absence is not certified.
- No row equates approval with execution, reviewer metadata with authenticated authority, proof presence with durable approval validity, cancellation with revocation, or timestamps with freshness.

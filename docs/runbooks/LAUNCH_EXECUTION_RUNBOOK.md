# Launch Execution Runbook

## 1. Launch Preparation
- Define campaign goal
- Select products
- Define audience
- Define offers

---

## 2. Content Production
- Create posts
- Generate visuals
- Prepare captions
- Prepare email content

---

## 3. Channel Packaging
- Adapt content per channel
- Create payloads
- Prepare publishing assets

---

## 4. Scheduling
- Assign schedule
- Queue jobs
- Validate readiness

---

## 5. Execution
- Execute jobs
- Publish manually (semi_auto)
- Send emails
- Monitor channels

---

## 6. Moderation
- Reply to comments
- Handle messages
- Track engagement

---

## 7. Performance Tracking
- Views
- CTR
- Clicks
- Conversions
- Saves

---

## 8. Optimization
- Review content
- Improve weak posts
- Repeat successful patterns

---

## 9. Post-Launch Actions
- Analyze campaign
- Identify winners
- Scale successful content
- Prepare next wave
